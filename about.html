Our Story

Niyantri Coffee began as a neighborhood dream—to create a space where coffee lovers feel at home. We source beans from small farms, roast in small batches, and design every detail to feel warm, welcoming, and a little playful. We believe good coffee brings people together, one cozy moment at a time.
